What do these numbers mean?

Alright, first, do you notice all of the percentages at the top? Those indicate how much percent an attack of the type listed on the left-hand column will do. Normal damage is 100%, "Super Effective" attacks are 200%, and "It's not very effective..." attacks are 50%.

Additionally, a Pokemon with two types can have a double Weakness or a double Resistance. Some Pokemon also have a full immunity to certain types.

This table here shows how many Pokemon on your team are weak or resistant to all of the different types. In each cell is listed how many Pokemon of the coresponding type take X% damage to the attack.

Any cell in a bright red means you should consider changing your team around, because you have a large weakness in that type!
